## Артикли со степенями сравнения

- It is a **cheaper** model.
- It is a **better** hotel.
- It is a **more expensive** hotel.
- It is a **much better** hotel.
- It's my **best** result.

## as ... as — такой же ..., как

- This course is <font color="#10b981">**as good as**</font> that one.
- That course <font color="#10b981">**isn't as good as**</font> this one.
- This house <font color="#10b981">**isn't as big as**</font> ours.
- This book <font color="#10b981">**isn't as interesting as**</font> that one.

## the ..., the ... — чем ..., тем ...

- <font color="#10b981">**The more, the better**</font>.
- <font color="#10b981">**The more**</font> practice, <font color="#10b981">**the better**</font>.
- <font color="#10b981">**The more**</font> examples, <font color="#10b981">**the better**</font>.
- <font color="#10b981">**The more**</font> practice we have, <font color="#10b981">**the better**</font> it is for us.
- <font color="#10b981">**The more**</font> I study, <font color="#10b981">**the more**</font> I understand.

## Полезные фразы

### the same age — одного возраста

- We are **the same age**.
- He is **the same age** as me.

### in the near future — в ближайшем будущем

- It can happen **in the near future**.
- It might happen **in the near future**.
- It may happen **in the near future**.
- I am waiting for **further** instructions.

### much + сравнительная степень

- This test is **much easier** than the previous one.
- This test is **much more difficult** than that one.

### one of the most ... — один из самых ...

- It is **one of the most important** things.
- He is **one of the richest** people in the world.
- It is **one of the biggest** cities in England.
- It is **one of the most difficult** things for understanding.

### one of the ... I have ever ... — один из самых ..., что я когда-либо ...

- It is **one of the most effective** ways of learning English.
- It is **one of the best** movies I have ever watched.
- It is **one of the most interesting** books I have ever read.
- It is **the most beautiful** place I have ever seen.
- It is **one of the most beautiful** places I have ever visited.
