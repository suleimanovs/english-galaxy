- It <font color="#10b981">**concerns**</font> each of us.

- I can't <font color="#10b981">**afford**</font> to buy it now.

- He can't afford to buy a new apartment <font color="#10b981">**at present**</font>.

- This country <font color="#10b981">**supplies**</font> oil and gas.
