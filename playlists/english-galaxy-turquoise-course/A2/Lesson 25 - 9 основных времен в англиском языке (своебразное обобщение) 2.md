## Введение в тему

Своеобразное обобщение девяти основных времён английского языка. Ниже — формулы, краткие пояснения и примеры для каждого времени, а в конце — смешанная практика.

## Времена

### Past Simple + Past Continuous

Сочетание двух прошедших времён в одном предложении: завершённое действие (Past Simple) и длительное действие, происходившее в тот момент (Past Continuous).

- When I <font color="#10b981">**opened**</font> the door, I <font color="#10b981">**saw**</font> that it <font color="#10b981">**was raining**</font>.
  - **Past Simple** — When I opened the door, I saw that…
  - **Past Continuous** — …it was raining (длительное действие).

### Present Simple

Используется для общих фактов и постоянных истин — вся фраза в Present Simple.

- Learning English <font color="#10b981">**seems**</font> so interesting.

### Present Continuous

Действие происходит прямо сейчас.

**Формула:** am / is / are + verb + ing

- I <font color="#10b981">**am watching**</font> a new series now.

### Present Perfect Continuous

Действие, начавшееся в прошлом и продолжающееся до настоящего.

**Формула:** have / has + been + verb + ing

- He <font color="#10b981">**has been learning**</font> English for three years.

### Present Perfect

Действия, произошедшие в прошлом, но результат важен сейчас.

**Формула:** have / has + verb (V3)

- He <font color="#10b981">**has**</font> just <font color="#10b981">**come**</font> home.
- I <font color="#10b981">**have**</font> never <font color="#10b981">**been**</font> to the USA, but I want to go there one day.
- I <font color="#10b981">**have**</font> already <font color="#10b981">**finished**</font> it.

### Past Perfect

Действия, произошедшие раньше других действий в прошлом.

**Формула:** had + verb (V3)

- She said that she <font color="#10b981">**had bought**</font> a very fashionable dress.

### Future Simple

> [!tip] If и after дают ограничение
> После If и after используем **Present Simple**, а не Future.

- If the weather <font color="#10b981">**doesn't change**</font>, I <font color="#10b981">**will stay**</font> at home.
  - If даёт ограничение, поэтому после If используем Present Simple.
- I <font color="#10b981">**will tell**</font> you my opinion after I <font color="#10b981">**read**</font> this article.
  - after даёт ограничение, поэтому после after используем Present Simple.

### Future Continuous

**Формула:** will be + verb + ing

- I <font color="#10b981">**will be waiting**</font> for your message.

## Смешанная практика

- I know it now.
- While she <font color="#10b981">**was sleeping**</font>, he <font color="#10b981">**was writing**</font> a report.
- I'm extremely exhausted, because I <font color="#10b981">**have been working**</font> all day.
- I <font color="#10b981">**was having**</font> dinner, when he <font color="#10b981">**called**</font> me.
- When <font color="#10b981">**did**</font> you <font color="#10b981">**lose**</font> it?
- He <font color="#10b981">**came**</font> home a minute ago.
- He <font color="#10b981">**passed**</font> the test because he <font color="#10b981">**had prepared**</font> for it very thoroughly.
- It was a very difficult choice for all of us.
- I <font color="#10b981">**will call**</font> you as soon as I <font color="#10b981">**finish**</font> it.
- I <font color="#10b981">**am thinking**</font> about it at the moment.
- I was in this city two days ago.
- What <font color="#10b981">**did**</font> you <font color="#10b981">**do**</font> yesterday?
- What <font color="#10b981">**were**</font> you <font color="#10b981">**doing**</font> yesterday at six o'clock in the evening?
- He can't answer now, because he <font color="#10b981">**is having**</font> a shower.
- The shop was closed so we went to go to another place.
