##### detailed — детальный, подробный

- They wrote a very <font color="#10b981">**detailed**</font> report.
- We got a very <font color="#10b981">**detailed**</font> answer to this question.

##### educational — образовательный

- He mostly prefers watching <font color="#10b981">**educational**</font> programs.

##### for educational purposes — в образовательных целях

- This information is used <font color="#10b981">**for educational purposes**</font>.

##### traditional — традиционный

- They trust <font color="#10b981">**traditional**</font> medicine more.

##### approach — подход

- It is a traditional <font color="#10b981">**approach**</font> to this problem.

##### satisfactory — удовлетворительный

- On average, the result is quite <font color="#10b981">**satisfactory**</font>.

##### lucky — везучий

- He is incredibly <font color="#10b981">**lucky**</font> in terms of money.

##### incredibly — невероятно

- He is <font color="#10b981">**incredibly**</font> lucky in terms of money.

##### in terms of — в отношении чего-то

- He is incredibly lucky <font color="#10b981">**in terms of**</font> money.

##### successful — успешный

- Overall, he is a very <font color="#10b981">**successful**</font> businessman.
- Overall, this project is quite <font color="#10b981">**successful**</font>.

##### Overall, … — В целом…

- <font color="#10b981">**Overall**</font>, he is a very successful businessman.
- <font color="#10b981">**Overall**</font>, this project is quite successful.

##### convinced — убеждён

- They were <font color="#10b981">**convinced**</font> that she was innocent.

##### innocent — невиновен

- They were convinced that she was <font color="#10b981">**innocent**</font>.

##### unable — неспособен

- The authorities are <font color="#10b981">**unable**</font> to change anything at the moment.

##### the authorities — власти

- <font color="#10b981">**The authorities**</font> are unable to change anything at the moment.

##### passive — пассивный

- His dream is having a small <font color="#10b981">**passive**</font> income.

##### reasonable — разумный

- I suppose that this price is quite <font color="#10b981">**reasonable**</font>.

##### Практика

- He has a completely different opinion.
