##### high — высокий, высота

- As I see it is a very <font color="#10b981">**high**</font> price.
- The boy jumped very <font color="#10b981">**high**</font>.
- It is very <font color="#10b981">**high**</font> here.

##### highly — высоко, крайне, весьма

> [!note] Запомни
> **highly** используется для усиления, а не для обозначения высоты или дистанции.

- It is a <font color="#10b981">**highly**</font> effective method.

##### highly appreciate — высоко ценить

- I <font color="#10b981">**highly appreciate**</font> your help.

##### nearly — почти

- He <font color="#10b981">**nearly**</font> passed the exam.
- I <font color="#10b981">**nearly**</font> achieved this aim.

##### fast — быстрый, быстро

- You shouldn't drive so <font color="#10b981">**fast**</font>.

##### Nevertheless — тем не менее

- <font color="#10b981">**Nevertheless,**</font> I managed to do it.
- <font color="#10b981">**Nevertheless,**</font> I managed to earn this money.
- <font color="#10b981">**Nevertheless,**</font> I preferred not to do it.

##### However — однако

- <font color="#10b981">**However,**</font> the situation isn't still stable.

##### slightly — немного, слегка

- It is a <font color="#10b981">**slightly**</font> informal answer.

##### partly — частично

- I <font color="#10b981">**partly**</font> agree with you.
- He <font color="#10b981">**partly**</font> agrees with this statement.

##### final — окончательный, финальный

- Is it your <font color="#10b981">**final**</font> decision?
- It is my <font color="#10b981">**final**</font> choice.

##### finally — наконец, наконец-то

- I <font color="#10b981">**finally**</font> got his permission.
- <font color="#10b981">**Finally,**</font> I got to this place.
- <font color="#10b981">**Finally,**</font> I finished my work.

##### Undoubtedly — несомненно

- <font color="#10b981">**Undoubtedly,**</font> it was a huge success.
- <font color="#10b981">**Undoubtedly,**</font> it is a real success.
- <font color="#10b981">**Undoubtedly,**</font> it was a real sensation.
