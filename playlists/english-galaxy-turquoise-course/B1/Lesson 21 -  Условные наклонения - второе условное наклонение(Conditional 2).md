## Введение в тему

**Conditional 2 (второе условное наклонение)** используется, когда мы говорим о чём-то **нереальном в настоящем времени** — о воображаемых ситуациях, мечтах и предположениях.

## Формула

- **If + Past Simple, would / could + verb**
- **I wish + Past Simple**

> [!tip] Was или were?
> В условных предложениях часто используют именно **were** (для всех лиц), но в разговорной речи **was** тоже встречается.

## Примеры

### If + Past Simple, would / could

- If I had enough money I <font color="#10b981">**would buy**</font> another house.
- If I lived in America my English <font color="#10b981">**would be**</font> much better.
- If I trained more I <font color="#10b981">**could show**</font> a much better result.
- If I <font color="#10b981">**was**</font> in your place I would do it.
- If I <font color="#10b981">**was**</font> you, I would do it.
- If I <font color="#10b981">**was**</font> rich I could travel around the world.
- If it <font color="#10b981">**was**</font> cheaper I would buy it.
- What would you do if you <font color="#10b981">**were**</font> in my place?
- What would you do if you had one billion dollars?
- If I was in your place I <font color="#10b981">**wouldn't worry**</font> about it so much.
- If I had enough money I would buy this car.
- If I was you I would do the same.
- If I was you I would do another thing.
- If I had one million dollars I would buy a new house.
- What would you do if you had two million dollars?

### I wish + Past Simple

- I wish I <font color="#10b981">**knew**</font> it. — Мне хотелось бы, чтобы я знал это.
- I wish it <font color="#10b981">**was**</font> possible.
- I wish I <font color="#10b981">**had**</font> this opportunity.
- I wish I had more money.
- I wish you <font color="#10b981">**were**</font> here.
- I wish I had more free time.
- I wish I had a higher salary.
