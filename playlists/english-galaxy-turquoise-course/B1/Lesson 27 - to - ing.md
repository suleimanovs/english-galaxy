## Введение в тему

После одних глаголов идёт инфинитив с **to**, а после других — форма с окончанием **-ing**. Важно запомнить, какой глагол какую конструкцию требует.

## Глаголы с инфинитивом (to + verb)

После таких глаголов, как **would like**, **hope**, **need**, **manage**, **advise**, **decide**, **be glad**, **fail**, используется инфинитив с **to**.

- I'd like <font color="#10b981">**to ask**</font> you some questions.
- He hopes <font color="#10b981">**to find**</font> a good lawyer.
- You need <font color="#10b981">**to look**</font> at the context.
- She managed <font color="#10b981">**to solve**</font> this problem in the end.
- What can you advise me <font color="#10b981">**to do**</font> in such a situation?
- We decided <font color="#10b981">**to go**</font> in another direction.
- I'm very glad <font color="#10b981">**to see**</font> you.
- They failed <font color="#10b981">**to resolve**</font> this conflict.

## Глаголы с герундием (verb + -ing)

После таких глаголов, как **recommend**, **consider** (рассматривать), **deny** (отрицать), **avoid**, **spend time**, **like**, **stop**, **give up**, используется форма с **-ing**.

- recommend **doing** something
- avoid **doing** something
- What do you recommend <font color="#10b981">**doing**</font> in this situation?
- He considered <font color="#10b981">**moving**</font> to the USA.
- She avoids <font color="#10b981">**talking**</font> about her past.
- He denies <font color="#10b981">**stealing**</font> this money.
- She spent lots of time on <font color="#10b981">**cooking**</font> this cake.
- Do you like <font color="#10b981">**driving**</font> a car?
- He stopped/gave up <font color="#10b981">**smoking**</font> several years ago.
- What can you recommend <font color="#10b981">**doing**</font> in such a situation?

## Практика

- What can you advise me **to do** in this situation?
- Would you like **to go** on a picnic now?
- To tell the truth, I don't like **getting up** early, but I have to do it.
