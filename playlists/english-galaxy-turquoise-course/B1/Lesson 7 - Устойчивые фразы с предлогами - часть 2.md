##### in general — в общем

- <font color="#10b981">**In general**</font>, it is an interesting offer.
- <font color="#10b981">**In general**</font>, it is quite an interesting offer.

##### in terms of — в отношении к чему-то

- I don't know what to do <font color="#10b981">**in terms of**</font> this problem.
- I want to tell you one thing <font color="#10b981">**in terms of**</font> this problem.

##### on good terms — в хороших отношениях

- I'm <font color="#10b981">**on good terms**</font> with my neighbours.

##### in fact — в действительности

- <font color="#10b981">**In fact**</font>, it isn't true.

##### in charge of — ответственен за

- He is <font color="#10b981">**in charge of**</font> this department.
- She is <font color="#10b981">**in charge of**</font> the marketing department.
- He is <font color="#10b981">**in charge of**</font> the sales department.

##### by nature — от природы

- She is a very talented person <font color="#10b981">**by nature**</font>.

##### to my surprise — к моему удивлению

- It didn't happen <font color="#10b981">**to my surprise**</font>.

##### to my great surprise — к моему большому удивлению

- It really happened <font color="#10b981">**to my great surprise**</font>.

##### to my greatest surprise — к моему огромному удивлению

##### take it into account — принимать это во внимание

- You need to <font color="#10b981">**take it into account**</font>.
- I need to <font color="#10b981">**take it into account**</font>.

##### in doubt — в сомнении

- I was <font color="#10b981">**in doubt**</font>.
- I'm <font color="#10b981">**in doubt**</font> now.

##### doubts about — сомнения по поводу чего-то

- I have some <font color="#10b981">**doubts about**</font> it.

##### in a hurry — торопиться / спешить

- Are you <font color="#10b981">**in a hurry**</font>?

##### in other words — другими словами

- <font color="#10b981">**In other words**</font>, you can also do it.
- <font color="#10b981">**In other words**</font>, this thing also deserves our attention.

##### on foot — пешком

- I often go there <font color="#10b981">**on foot**</font>.

##### for the time being — пока, на данный момент

- I want to concentrate/focus on this problem <font color="#10b981">**for the time being**</font>.

##### in addition to it — в добавление к этому

- <font color="#10b981">**In addition to it**</font>, it can lead to more serious problems.

##### in good condition — в хорошем состоянии

- The car is <font color="#10b981">**in good condition**</font>.

##### in a good mood — в хорошем настроении

- I'm <font color="#10b981">**in a good mood**</font>.

##### take it for granted — принимать это как должное

- You can't <font color="#10b981">**take it for granted**</font>.
- Most people <font color="#10b981">**take it for granted**</font>.

##### on the contrary — наоборот

- <font color="#10b981">**On the contrary**</font>, we need to pay attention to it.
