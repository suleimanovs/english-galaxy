## Полезная лексика

##### due to · because of — из-за

- Their flight was delayed <font color="#10b981">**due to**</font> bad weather.
- His flight was cancelled <font color="#10b981">**due to**</font> bad weather conditions.

##### due to bad weather — из-за плохой погоды

##### a sense of admiration — чувство восхищения

- He said it with <font color="#10b981">**a sense of admiration**</font>.

## Времена в английском

### Present Simple

- I <font color="#10b981">**don't often do**</font> it.
- He <font color="#10b981">**doesn't often go**</font> there.
- It <font color="#10b981">**is said**</font>, it is one of the best hotels in the world.

### Present Continuous

- She <font color="#10b981">**is talking**</font> on the phone right now.
- She <font color="#10b981">**is still waiting**</font> for you.

### Present Perfect

- <font color="#10b981">**Have**</font> you ever <font color="#10b981">**been**</font> to Spain?
- I <font color="#10b981">**have never been**</font> there before.
- I <font color="#10b981">**have been**</font> busy with it lately.
- It is the funniest video I <font color="#10b981">**have ever watched**</font>.

### Present Perfect Continuous

- He <font color="#10b981">**has been living**</font> in New York for the last five years.
- How long <font color="#10b981">**have**</font> you <font color="#10b981">**been living**</font> abroad?
- I <font color="#10b981">**have been waiting**</font> for his call since morning.
- How long <font color="#10b981">**have**</font> you <font color="#10b981">**been playing**</font> this game?

### Past Simple

- He <font color="#10b981">**said**</font> it with a sense of admiration.
- What <font color="#10b981">**stopped**</font> him?
- I <font color="#10b981">**had to do**</font> it in any case.
- I <font color="#10b981">**was**</font> in Spain many years ago.
- I <font color="#10b981">**saw**</font> a great movie yesterday.
- I <font color="#10b981">**don't know**</font> what to start with.
- It <font color="#10b981">**was**</font> very difficult to understand what to start with.

### Past Continuous

- The sun <font color="#10b981">**was shining**</font> and it was very warm.
- He <font color="#10b981">**was doing**</font> his homework while his brother <font color="#10b981">**was playing**</font> a computer game.
- When the teacher <font color="#10b981">**was trying**</font> to explain something no one <font color="#10b981">**was listening**</font> to her.
- We <font color="#10b981">**were watching**</font> this program, when we heard a strange noise.
- I hope I will be able to speak English fluently next year.

### Past Simple Passive

- Their flight <font color="#10b981">**was delayed**</font> due to bad weather.
- His flight <font color="#10b981">**was cancelled**</font> due to bad weather conditions.

### Future Simple

- I <font color="#10b981">**will finish**</font> writing this letter in about five minutes.

### Future Perfect

- I expect that I <font color="#10b981">**will have reached**</font> the next level by next month.
