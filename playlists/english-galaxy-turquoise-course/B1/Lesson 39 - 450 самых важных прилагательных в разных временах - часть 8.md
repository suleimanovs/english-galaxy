##### private — частный

- It is <font color="#10b981">**private**</font> property.

##### polite to — быть вежливым к кому-то

- You need to be more <font color="#10b981">**polite to**</font> others.

##### inexpensive — недорогой

- It is a very <font color="#10b981">**inexpensive**</font> device.
- This thing is relatively <font color="#10b981">**inexpensive**</font>.

##### fair — справедливый

- It seems to me that it isn't a <font color="#10b981">**fair**</font> decision.
- I suppose this decision is <font color="#10b981">**unfair**</font>.

##### aggressive — агрессивный

- They can start taking more <font color="#10b981">**aggressive**</font> measures.

##### medical · medical treatment · medical care — медицинский · медицинское лечение · медицинский уход

- The patient receives very good <font color="#10b981">**medical treatment**</font>.
- This patient needs good <font color="#10b981">**medical care**</font>.

##### primary — главный, основной

- It is our <font color="#10b981">**primary**</font> aim.
- It is our <font color="#10b981">**main**</font> aim.

##### amazed (by/at) — поражён

- I was really <font color="#10b981">**amazed by**</font> the beauty of this place.
- We were really <font color="#10b981">**amazed by**</font> those words.

##### previous — предыдущий

- It was mentioned in the <font color="#10b981">**previous**</font> lesson.

##### cautious — осторожный

- It is necessary to be more <font color="#10b981">**cautious**</font>.

##### faithful · faithful to — верный

- He remained <font color="#10b981">**faithful to**</font> his principles.

> [!note] Однокоренное слово
> **faith** — вера.

##### negative — отрицательный, негативный

- It can lead to very <font color="#10b981">**negative**</font> consequences.
- These changes may have a very <font color="#10b981">**negative**</font> effect on our economy.

> [!note] Однокоренное слово
> **consequences** — последствия.

##### exclusive — эксклюзивный

- Does this company have <font color="#10b981">**exclusive**</font> rights?

##### uninterested · uninterested in — не заинтересован

- He was <font color="#10b981">**uninterested in**</font> that project.
