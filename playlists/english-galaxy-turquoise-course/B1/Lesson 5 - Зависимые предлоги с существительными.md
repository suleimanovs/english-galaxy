##### attitude to / towards — отношение к
- What is your <font color="#10b981">**attitude to**</font> it?
- He has a very negative <font color="#10b981">**attitude to**</font> smoking.

##### influence something — влиять на что-то (глагол)
- This event <font color="#10b981">**influenced**</font> my life greatly.

##### influence on something — влияние на что-то (сущ.)
- This event had a very great <font color="#10b981">**influence on**</font> my life.

##### lack something — не хватать чего-то
- She <font color="#10b981">**lacks**</font> work experience.
- He <font color="#10b981">**lacks**</font> confidence.

##### a lack of something — недостаток, нехватка чего-то
- He has a <font color="#10b981">**lack of**</font> experience.
- He has a <font color="#10b981">**lack of**</font> confidence.

##### apologise for — извиняться за
- She <font color="#10b981">**apologised for**</font> being late.

##### divide into — разделять на
- I <font color="#10b981">**divided**</font> my presentation <font color="#10b981">**into**</font> three parts.

##### answer something — answer как глагол
- I haven't <font color="#10b981">**answered**</font> this letter yet.

##### answer to something — answer как существительное
- What is the <font color="#10b981">**answer to**</font> this question?
- It is the best <font color="#10b981">**answer to**</font> that question.

##### reason for — причина чего-то
- What is the <font color="#10b981">**reason for**</font> it?

##### recover from — выздороветь после / оправиться от
- It will take him some time to <font color="#10b981">**recover from**</font> the operation.

##### alternative to — альтернатива чего-то
- It seems to me, it is a good <font color="#10b981">**alternative to**</font> that offer.
- It is a good <font color="#10b981">**alternative to**</font> this method.

##### demand — спрос
##### demand for — спрос на
- The <font color="#10b981">**demand for**</font> this product is very high.
- The <font color="#10b981">**demand for**</font> this product became/got lower.

##### solution to — решение чего-то
- It is the best <font color="#10b981">**solution to**</font> these problems.
- I think that it is the best <font color="#10b981">**solution to**</font> such a difficult problem.

##### refer to — ссылаться на
- He often <font color="#10b981">**refers to**</font> these quotes.
- He always <font color="#10b981">**refers to**</font> these sources.

##### quote — цитата
##### source — источник

##### insist on — настаивать на
- He <font color="#10b981">**insisted on**</font> doing it.

##### way of — способ чего-то
- It is one of the most effective <font color="#10b981">**ways of**</font> learning foreign languages.

##### I come to the conclusion that — я прихожу к выводу что
- <font color="#10b981">**I come to the conclusion that**</font> it is the most effective way of learning foreign languages.
