##### stylish — стильный

- She bought a very <font color="#10b981">**stylish**</font> dress.

##### energetic — энергичный

- He is such an <font color="#10b981">**energetic**</font> person.

##### economic — экономический

- It is a very serious <font color="#10b981">**economic**</font> problem.

##### aware — осведомлён · aware of — осведомлён о чём-то

- I'm <font color="#10b981">**aware of**</font> these problems.

##### unique — уникальный

- It is a <font color="#10b981">**unique**</font> chance and I can't miss it.
- It is a <font color="#10b981">**unique**</font> opportunity and I can't miss it.

##### temporary — временный

- It is a <font color="#10b981">**temporary**</font> solution to this problem.

##### arrogant — высокомерный

- This fame made him very rude and <font color="#10b981">**arrogant**</font>.

##### fame — слава

- This <font color="#10b981">**fame**</font> made him very rude and arrogant.

##### rude — грубый

- This fame made him very <font color="#10b981">**rude**</font> and arrogant.

##### careful — осторожный

- He is a very <font color="#10b981">**careful**</font> driver.

##### careless — неосторожный, беззаботный

- He is such a <font color="#10b981">**careless**</font> driver.
- I think he needs to stop being so <font color="#10b981">**careless**</font>.

##### inevitable — неизбежный

- I think that the success of this project is <font color="#10b981">**inevitable**</font>.
- This crisis seems almost <font color="#10b981">**inevitable**</font> now.

##### opposite — напротив, наоборот · the opposite … — противоположный

- That expression has the <font color="#10b981">**opposite**</font> meaning.

##### sensible — разумный

- From my point of view it is a very <font color="#10b981">**sensible**</font> idea.
- In my opinion this decision is quite <font color="#10b981">**sensible**</font>.

##### Практика

- It is obvious.
- It is obvious enough.
- She is only interested in material things.
