## Полезная лексика

- **put on weight** · **gain weight** — набрать вес
- **get free** — освободиться
- **hurry up** — торопиться

## Conditional 0 (нулевой тип)

Используем, когда говорим о том, что вообще (в целом) происходит.

> [!tip] Формула
> **If + Present Simple, Present Simple**

- If I'm tired <font color="#10b981">**I go**</font> to bed early.
- If we eat too much we <font color="#10b981">**put on**</font> weight.
- If he has free time he usually reads something for pleasure.
- If I'm not busy I usually devote my free time to it.

## Conditional 1 (первый тип)

Используем, когда говорим о будущем.

> [!tip] Формула
> **If / When / As soon as / After / Before / In case + Present Simple, Future Simple**

> [!note]
> If даёт ограничение, хотя мы говорим о будущем, потому первая часть после If — это Present Simple.

- If it is sunny we <font color="#10b981">**will go**</font> to the park.
- If I wake up late I <font color="#10b981">**will miss**</font> the bus.
- I will go there with you if you ask me.
- When my brother returns home I will tell you.
- I will call you when I finish work.
- Let me know when you get free.
- I will spend more time on English if I have free time.
- Let me know if you need my help.
- Call me as soon as you get free.
- He will start working after he graduates from university.
- I will come to you one more time before I leave the city.

## Смешанная практика

- You need to hurry up if you don't want to be late.
- If you don't hurry up you will miss the bus.
- I will do it if I have time.
- You will see this building on the right when you cross the next street.
- He will get money after he completes this project.
- If I have enough money I will buy this thing.
- I will call you in case I need your help.
- He says that if it rains he will stay at home.
- What will you do if this thing happens?
- When he finishes working on this project he will go home.
