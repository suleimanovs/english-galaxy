##### as ... as — такой же ... как (равенство)

- He is <font color="#10b981">**as smart as**</font> his sister.
- This book isn't <font color="#10b981">**as interesting as**</font> that one.
- This thing isn't <font color="#10b981">**as expensive as**</font> that one.
- This car isn't <font color="#10b981">**as old as**</font> that one.

##### Артикли в сравнительных конструкциях

- It is <font color="#10b981">**a better**</font> decision.
- It is <font color="#10b981">**a much better**</font> choice.
- It's <font color="#10b981">**a more serious**</font> economic problem.
- I suppose it is <font color="#10b981">**a much better**</font> decision.
- This bag is <font color="#10b981">**three times cheaper than**</font> that one.

##### one of — один из

- It is <font color="#10b981">**one of**</font> the most common English words.
- He is <font color="#10b981">**one of**</font> the richest people in the world.
- It is <font color="#10b981">**one of**</font> the most serious problems.
- It is <font color="#10b981">**one of**</font> the most interesting recent discoveries.

##### than this one — чем этот

- That presentation is much more colourful <font color="#10b981">**than this one**</font>.

##### the same ... as — такой же ... как (тождество)

- I have <font color="#10b981">**the same**</font> questions <font color="#10b981">**as**</font> you.
- He has <font color="#10b981">**the same**</font> mistakes <font color="#10b981">**as**</font> you.

##### Местоимения в сравнительных конструкциях

- My level is higher than <font color="#10b981">**his**</font>.
- His level is lower than <font color="#10b981">**mine**</font>.
- Your idea is the same as <font color="#10b981">**hers**</font>.
- Our house is smaller than <font color="#10b981">**theirs**</font>.

##### the ..., the ... — чем ..., тем ...

- <font color="#10b981">**The**</font> sooner, <font color="#10b981">**the**</font> better.
- <font color="#10b981">**The**</font> more practice you have, <font color="#10b981">**the**</font> better it is for you.
- <font color="#10b981">**The**</font> more I think about it, <font color="#10b981">**the**</font> more I like this idea.
- <font color="#10b981">**The**</font> more you study, <font color="#10b981">**the**</font> more you know.
- <font color="#10b981">**The**</font> more we have, <font color="#10b981">**the**</font> more we want.
