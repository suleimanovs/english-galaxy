## Предложения с if

Используется, когда ответ должен быть **да/нет**. **If** играет роль «ли», например «живёт ли?».

- Do you know <font color="#10b981">**if**</font> she lives there?
- He asked me <font color="#10b981">**if**</font> I had any questions.
- She asked me <font color="#10b981">**if**</font> I was busy.

## Предложения с whether

Используется, когда в конце есть уточнение: **whether... or not?**

- I don't know <font color="#10b981">**whether**</font> she is at home or not.
- I don't know <font color="#10b981">**whether**</font> they will agree or not.

## Согласование времён

После прошедшего времени в главном предложении используется **Past Perfect**.

- He asked me if I <font color="#10b981">**had** ever **been**</font> there. *(Past Perfect)*
- He said that she <font color="#10b981">**had** never **been**</font> there. *(Past Perfect)*
- He asked me if I <font color="#10b981">**had** ever **been**</font> to this place. *(Past Perfect)*

## Future in the Past

В косвенной речи и при согласовании времён **will** меняется на **would**.

- I knew that it <font color="#10b981">**would**</font> happen.
- I thought that he <font color="#10b981">**would**</font> be late.
- I told her that they <font color="#10b981">**wouldn't**</font> believe it.

> [!tip] Когда if, а когда whether
> **If** — используем, когда есть выбор да/нет.
> **Whether** — используем, когда есть уточнение «... or not?».

- He asked me <font color="#10b981">**if**</font> I had been to England before.
- I don't know <font color="#10b981">**if**</font> I should do it.
- I don't know <font color="#10b981">**if**</font> he is at home.
- Tell me where this place is.
- He asked me <font color="#10b981">**if**</font> I was busy.
- She said that she <font color="#10b981">**would**</font> come to the party. *(will заменён на would)*
- I wasn't sure <font color="#10b981">**if**</font> it was the right decision.
- I don't know how far this place is.
