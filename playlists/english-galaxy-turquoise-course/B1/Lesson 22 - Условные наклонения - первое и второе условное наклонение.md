## First Conditional

> [!tip] Формула
> **If + Present Simple, Future Simple**
> **When + Present Simple**

### Примеры

- If it happens I <font color="#10b981">**will be**</font> happy.
- If I win the lottery I <font color="#10b981">**will buy**</font> something for myself.
- If it rains I <font color="#10b981">**won't go**</font> to the park.
- I'll <font color="#10b981">**tell**</font> you when I'm ready.
- He'll <font color="#10b981">**be**</font> very surprised when he knows it.
- If he earns enough money he <font color="#10b981">**will go**</font> to America.
- If you are busy I <font color="#10b981">**won't disturb**</font> you.
- If you need my help <font color="#10b981">**let me know**</font>.
- If you need my support <font color="#10b981">**let me know**</font>.
- What <font color="#10b981">**will you do**</font> if it happens?

## Second Conditional

> [!tip] Формула
> **If + Past Simple + would/could + verb**
> **If only + Past Simple** — если бы только + Past Simple

### Примеры

- If he had more money he <font color="#10b981">**would buy**</font> a new house.
- If I <font color="#10b981">**were**</font> you, I would accept/take this offer.
- I wish I <font color="#10b981">**had**</font> a better memory.
- I wish I <font color="#10b981">**could spend**</font> more time on it.
- <font color="#10b981">**If only**</font> I knew it.
- <font color="#10b981">**If only**</font> I had more free time.
- <font color="#10b981">**If only**</font> I had this knowledge.
- If I <font color="#10b981">**were**</font> rich my life would be completely different.
- I wish I <font color="#10b981">**could do**</font> it.
- <font color="#10b981">**If only**</font> you were here.
- I wish there <font color="#10b981">**were**</font> twenty five hours in a day.
- <font color="#10b981">**If only**</font> I had one more chance.
- <font color="#10b981">**If only**</font> I could afford to buy it.
