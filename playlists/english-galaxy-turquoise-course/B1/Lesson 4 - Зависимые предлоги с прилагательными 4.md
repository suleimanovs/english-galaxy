##### depend on — зависеть от

- It will <font color="#10b981">**depend on**</font> him.
- What does it <font color="#10b981">**depend on**</font>?
- What will it <font color="#10b981">**depend on**</font>?

##### answer something — отвечать на что-то

- How did you <font color="#10b981">**answer**</font> it?
- How did you <font color="#10b981">**answer**</font> this question?

##### reply to — отвечать на (более формально)

- Did she <font color="#10b981">**reply to**</font> your letter?

##### explain to — объяснять кому-то

- Can you <font color="#10b981">**explain**</font> it <font color="#10b981">**to**</font> me?
- I can <font color="#10b981">**explain**</font> this thing <font color="#10b981">**to**</font> you.

##### arrive — прибывать, приезжать

- arrive <font color="#10b981">**at**</font> the party — приехать на вечеринку
- arrive <font color="#10b981">**at**</font> the meeting — приехать на встречу
- arrive <font color="#10b981">**at**</font> the airport — приехать в аэропорт
- He <font color="#10b981">**arrived at**</font> the conference an hour later.

##### influence — влиять, влиять на, повлиять на

- It <font color="#10b981">**influenced**</font> me a lot.
- It <font color="#10b981">**influenced**</font> my life greatly.
- These events <font color="#10b981">**influenced**</font> my decision.

##### devote to — посвящать чему-то

- He <font color="#10b981">**devotes**</font> too **much** time <font color="#10b981">**to**</font> this hobby.

##### look for — искать

- What is he <font color="#10b981">**looking for**</font>?

##### react to — реагировать на

- How did she <font color="#10b981">**react to**</font> your words?

##### in the end — в конце

- He arrived <font color="#10b981">**in the end**</font>.

##### in the beginning — в начале

- She mentioned it <font color="#10b981">**in the beginning**</font>.

##### at the end of something — в конце чего-то

- He arrived <font color="#10b981">**at the end of**</font> the party.

##### at the beginning of something — в начале чего-то

- She mentioned it <font color="#10b981">**at the beginning of**</font> the lecture.

##### lead to — привести к чему-то

- I firmly believe that hard work can <font color="#10b981">**lead**</font> us <font color="#10b981">**to**</font> success.

##### adapt to — приспосабливаться к

- I had to <font color="#10b981">**adapt to**</font> those conditions.
- I have already <font color="#10b981">**adapted to**</font> these conditions.

##### listen to — слушать

- What music do you <font color="#10b981">**listen to**</font>?
- What kind of music do you <font color="#10b981">**listen to**</font>?

##### I firmly believe — я твёрдо верю

- I <font color="#10b981">**firmly believe**</font> that hard work can lead us to success.
