## Введение в тему

Пассивный залог (Passive Voice) используется, когда важно действие, а не тот, кто его совершает.

> [!note] Полезное выражение
> **all over the world** — по всему миру

## Формула

**форма глагола to be + 3 форма глагола (Past Participle)**

| Время | Форма | Название |
| --- | --- | --- |
| am/is/are written | Present Simple | Present Simple Passive |
| was/were written | Past Simple | Past Simple Passive |
| will be written | Future Simple | Future Simple Passive |

## Present Simple Passive

- This problem <font color="#10b981">**is discussed**</font> very often.
- English <font color="#10b981">**is spoken**</font> all over the world.
- It <font color="#10b981">**is said**</font> that it may happen sooner or later.

## Past Simple Passive

- This file <font color="#10b981">**was deleted**</font> by him.
- The money <font color="#10b981">**was stolen**</font>.
- The criminals <font color="#10b981">**were arrested**</font>.

## Future Simple Passive

- It <font color="#10b981">**will be done**</font> on time.
- This problem <font color="#10b981">**will be solved**</font>.
- These mistakes <font color="#10b981">**will be corrected**</font>.

## Смешанная практика

- He <font color="#10b981">**was seen**</font> at the party.
- These workers <font color="#10b981">**were replaced**</font>.
- He <font color="#10b981">**was invited**</font> to the party.
- All the participants <font color="#10b981">**were awarded**</font>.
- This mistake <font color="#10b981">**was corrected**</font>.
- The mistakes <font color="#10b981">**were corrected**</font>.
- The keys <font color="#10b981">**were found**</font> later.
- It <font color="#10b981">**is expected**</font> that he will approve of this idea.
- It <font color="#10b981">**is reported**</font> that two people were injured during the accident.
- Too little <font color="#10b981">**is done**</font> to improve the situation.
- Nothing <font color="#10b981">**was corrected**</font>.
- Not enough <font color="#10b981">**is done**</font> to resolve this conflict.
- Too little <font color="#10b981">**was done**</font> to resolve this conflict.
- Not enough <font color="#10b981">**was done**</font> to improve the situation.
- It <font color="#10b981">**is expected**</font> that they will win.
- It <font color="#10b981">**is expected**</font> that they will find a solution to this problem.
- Spanish <font color="#10b981">**is spoken**</font> in some countries.
- Our flight <font color="#10b981">**was delayed**</font>.
- He is often criticized for it.
- I'm sure that it <font color="#10b981">**will be done**</font>.
