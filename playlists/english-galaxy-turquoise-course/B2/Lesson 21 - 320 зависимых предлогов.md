##### oppose to · opposed to — возражать против / быть против кого-то, чего-то

- I strongly <font color="#10b981">**oppose to**</font> this decision.
- Most workers are <font color="#10b981">**opposed to**</font> these changes.
- Most employees are strongly <font color="#10b981">**opposed to**</font> these radical changes.

---

##### late for — опаздывать куда-то

- He was <font color="#10b981">**late for**</font> the meeting.
- He was <font color="#10b981">**late for**</font> the train.

---

##### thirst for — жажда чего-то

- I see that these students have a <font color="#10b981">**thirst for**</font> knowledge.

---

##### related to — связан с чем-то

- These two things are closely <font color="#10b981">**related to**</font> each other.

---

##### connected with — связан с чем-то

- These two problems are closely <font color="#10b981">**connected with**</font> each other.

---

##### connection between — связь между

- What is the <font color="#10b981">**connection between**</font> these two things?

---

##### in connection with — в связи с

- I'm writing to you <font color="#10b981">**in connection with**</font> your job offer.
- I'm writing to you <font color="#10b981">**in connection with**</font> your recent request.

---

##### prepare for — готовиться к чему-то

- I need to <font color="#10b981">**prepare for**</font> this test more thoroughly.

---

##### fond of — любить что-то

- She is <font color="#10b981">**fond of**</font> visiting various museums and art galleries.

---

##### ashamed of — стыдиться чего-то (shame — стыд, позор)

- He isn't <font color="#10b981">**ashamed of**</font> it.

##### near — около, рядом

- The hotel is located <font color="#10b981">**near**</font> the sea.
- The hotel is <font color="#10b981">**near**</font> the ocean.

---

##### close to — рядом с

- Their house is <font color="#10b981">**close to**</font> the forest.
- Her apartment / flat is <font color="#10b981">**close to**</font> the park.

---

##### obvious to — очевидно для

- It is <font color="#10b981">**obvious to**</font> me.
- It is <font color="#10b981">**obvious to**</font> most people.
