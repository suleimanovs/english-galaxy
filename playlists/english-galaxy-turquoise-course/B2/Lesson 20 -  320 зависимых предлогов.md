##### doubt · doubts about (something) — сомневаться, сомнения · сомнения по поводу чего-то
- I <font color="#10b981">**doubt**</font> it.
- I really <font color="#10b981">**doubt**</font> it.
- I seriously <font color="#10b981">**doubt**</font> it.
- I have serious <font color="#10b981">**doubts about**</font> the success of this project.
- There is no <font color="#10b981">**doubt about**</font> it. — В этом нет никаких сомнений.

> [!tip] Особый кейс: «There is no doubt about it» — выяснить, почему используется именно эта конструкция.

---

##### need for — необходимость чего-то
- He <font color="#10b981">**doubts the need for**</font> these changes.

---

##### specialize in — специализироваться на чем-то
- This company <font color="#10b981">**specializes in**</font> online education.
- My friend <font color="#10b981">**specializes in**</font> marketing.

---

##### dedicate to — уделять / посвящать чему-то
- I want to <font color="#10b981">**dedicate**</font> more time <font color="#10b981">**to**</font> my hobbies.
- I'm not ready to <font color="#10b981">**dedicate**</font> my life <font color="#10b981">**to**</font> it.
- He <font color="#10b981">**dedicated**</font> himself <font color="#10b981">**to**</font> science.

---

##### refer to — ссылаться на что-то
- He often <font color="#10b981">**refers to**</font> these documents.
- He <font color="#10b981">**referred to**</font> reliable sources.
- They often <font color="#10b981">**refer to**</font> these documents.

---

##### influence · influence on — влиять на, влияние (сущ. имеет предлог)
- This example <font color="#10b981">**influenced**</font> my life greatly.
- What <font color="#10b981">**influenced**</font> your decision?
- It has a bad <font color="#10b981">**influence on**</font> people.

---

##### hopeless at — безнадежен в чем-то
- It is said that I'm <font color="#10b981">**hopeless at**</font> dancing.
- As I know she is absolutely <font color="#10b981">**hopeless at**</font> singing.

---

##### nervous about — нервничает по поводу чего-то
- He is very <font color="#10b981">**nervous about**</font> the test.
- She is very <font color="#10b981">**nervous about**</font> the interview.

---

##### furnished with — обставлен чем-то
- Her room was <font color="#10b981">**furnished with**</font> antique furniture.

---

##### reluctant to — нежелающий делать что-то
- He is <font color="#10b981">**reluctant to**</font> share this information with others.
- They are <font color="#10b981">**reluctant to**</font> share their knowledge with others.
