форма глагола to be + 3 форма глагола

am/is/are written - Present Simple Passive
was/were written - Past Simple Passive
will be written - Future Simple Passive

#### Present Simple Passive

This problem <font color="#4f81bd">is</font> <font color="#4f81bd">discussed</font> very often 
English <font color="#4f81bd">is spoken</font> all over the world
It <font color="#4f81bd">is</font> <font color="#4f81bd">said</font> that, it may happen sooner or later

The criminals <font color="#b2a2c7">were arrested </font>
