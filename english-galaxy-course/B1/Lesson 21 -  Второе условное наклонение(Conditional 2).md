#### Conditional 2
*Используется когда что-то не реальное в настоящем времени*
*If + Past Simple, would / could + verb*
*I wish + Past Simple*

If I had enough money i would buy another house
If I lived in America my English would be much better
If i trained more i could show a much better result 
I wish i knew it - Мне хотелось бы, чтобы я знал это
I wish it was(<font color="#e36c09">часто используют именно were, но was тоже встречается</font>) possible 
I wish i had this opportunity
If I was(<font color="#e36c09">часто используют именно were, но was тоже встречается</font>) in your place i would do it 
If I was you, I would do it 
If I was rich I could travel around the world 
If it was cheaper I would buy it
What would you do if you were in my place?
What would you do if you had one billion dollars?
