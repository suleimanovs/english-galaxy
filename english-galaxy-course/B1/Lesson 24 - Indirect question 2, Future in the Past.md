#### Предложения с if 
Do you know, if she lives there? - если ответ должен быть да/нет, то используются if
if играет роль "ли", например "живет ли?"
He asked me if i had any questions
She asked me if i was busy

#### Предложения с whether 
Используется когда в конце есть уточнения: whether... or not?
I don't know whether she is at home or not?
I don't know whether they will agree or not? 

#### Согласование времен
He asked me if I had ever been there - Past Perfect
He said that she had never been there - Past Perfect
He asked me if i had ever been to this place - Past Perfect


#### Future in the Past
I knew that it would happen - will меняется на would 
I thought that he would be late
I told her that they wouldn't believe it
