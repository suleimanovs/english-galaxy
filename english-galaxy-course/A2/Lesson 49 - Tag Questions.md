 1.Present Simple - обычный глагол - don't/doesn't

You know it, don't you? -  Ты знаешь это, не так ли?
здесь "don't you?" является tag question

You learn English by yourself, don't you?
He really wants it, doesn't he? 

It seems really unbelievable, doesn't it?
It doesn't help, does it? - если утвержение через отрицание, то далее уже либо do либо does

These things don't help, do  they?

 2.Present Simple - форма глагол to be- isn't/arent
You are really happy, aren't you?
You are joking, aren't you?

Learning English is very interesting, isn't it?
It is really unbelievable, isn't it?
I'm right, aren't I? - почему здесь aren't?


 3.Past Simple - обычный глагол - didn't 

I did the right thing, didn't I?
He faced very serious problems, didn't he?
He didn't go there, did he?
She didn't help you, did she?

 4.Past Simple -форма глагол to be- wasn't/weren't
She was late for the meeting, wasn't she? 
You were late for the meeting, weren't you?
You weren't late, were you? 


5.Feature Simple - обычный глагол - won't

He will be with you, won't he?
We won't know about it, will we?
