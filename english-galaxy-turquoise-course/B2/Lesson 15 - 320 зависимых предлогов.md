##### harmful to — вредно для

- It is very <font color="#10b981">**harmful to**</font> the environment.
- It can be very <font color="#10b981">**harmful to**</font> our health.

##### interested in — интересующийся (в чём-то)

- She is particularly <font color="#10b981">**interested in**</font> photography.

##### focus on — фокусировать на, сконцентрироваться на, сосредоточиться на

- We need to <font color="#10b981">**focus on**</font> more significant problems.

##### inform of / about — информировать о чём-то, сообщить о чём-то

- Please, <font color="#10b981">**inform**</font> us <font color="#10b981">**about**</font> these changes.
- He was <font color="#10b981">**informed of**</font> it.

##### leave for — уезжать куда-то

- She <font color="#10b981">**left for**</font> London.
- I have already <font color="#10b981">**left for**</font> work.

##### covered with — покрыт чем-то

- His shoes are <font color="#10b981">**covered with**</font> dirt.

##### lead to — привести к чему-то

- It <font color="#10b981">**led to**</font> new unforeseen problems.

##### concentrate on — концентрироваться на

- We need to <font color="#10b981">**concentrate**</font> our attention <font color="#10b981">**on**</font> a more important thing.

##### result in — закончиться чем-то, привести к чему-то

- These measures <font color="#10b981">**resulted in**</font> very big losses.
- Unfortunately, all these attempts <font color="#10b981">**resulted in**</font> a complete failure.

##### react to — реагировать на

- He <font color="#10b981">**reacted**</font> very angrily <font color="#10b981">**to**</font> this remark.
- In your place, I wouldn't <font color="#10b981">**react to**</font> it.
- If I were you, I wouldn't <font color="#10b981">**react to**</font> it.

##### exhausted from · utterly exhausted — измучен чем-то · совершенно измучен

- She is absolutely <font color="#10b981">**exhausted from**</font> working all day without any breaks.
- He is <font color="#10b981">**utterly exhausted from**</font> such a tight schedule.

##### look forward to — ждать с нетерпением

- I'm <font color="#10b981">**looking forward to**</font> this party.
- I <font color="#10b981">**look forward to**</font> our meeting.
- I <font color="#10b981">**look forward to**</font> meeting you.

##### way of — способ чего-то

- There is another possible <font color="#10b981">**way of**</font> solving this problem.
- It is the most effective <font color="#10b981">**way of**</font> learning foreign languages.
