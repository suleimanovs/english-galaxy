##### trouble with — проблемы с чем-то

- He has <font color="#10b981">**trouble with**</font> his car.
- I had very big <font color="#10b981">**trouble with**</font> it.

##### cope with — справляться с чем-то

- I'm trying to <font color="#10b981">**cope with**</font> all this work.
- Unfortunately, he can't <font color="#10b981">**cope with**</font> this task.

##### addicted to — зависим от / пристрастился к чему-то

- He is <font color="#10b981">**addicted to**</font> surfing the internet.
- She is <font color="#10b981">**addicted to**</font> surfing the web.

##### difference between — разница между

- There is a big <font color="#10b981">**difference between**</font> these two things.
- There is not a big <font color="#10b981">**difference between**</font> these two products.

##### sensitive to — чувствителен к

- He is very <font color="#10b981">**sensitive to**</font> criticism.

##### unaware of — не осведомлён о чём-то / не в курсе чего-то

> [!note] totally unaware — совершенно не осведомлён / не в курсе

- We were <font color="#10b981">**unaware of**</font> these potential risks.
- He is totally <font color="#10b981">**unaware of**</font> this problem.
- As far as I can see she is totally <font color="#10b981">**unaware of**</font> what is happening here.

##### fall in — падение чего-то

- The company is experiencing a temporary <font color="#10b981">**fall in**</font> sales.
- There was a sharp <font color="#10b981">**fall in**</font> demand for these products.

##### innocent of — не виновен в чём-то

- He is <font color="#10b981">**innocent of**</font> this crime.

##### glance at — взглянуть на что-то

- I <font color="#10b981">**glanced at**</font> my watch to check the time.

##### work on — работать над чем-то

- I'm going to go on <font color="#10b981">**working on**</font> this project.
- He wanted to go on <font color="#10b981">**working on**</font> this issue.

##### criticise for — критиковать за

- She was <font color="#10b981">**criticized for**</font> it very sharply.
- They were <font color="#10b981">**criticized for**</font> these actions.

##### tremble with — дрожать от чего-то

- He was <font color="#10b981">**trembling with**</font> fear.
- Her voice was <font color="#10b981">**trembling with**</font> excitement.
