## Артикли в сравнительных конструкциях

- As far as I know, it is <font color="#10b981">**a cheaper**</font> hotel.
- It is <font color="#10b981">**a longer**</font> way.
- He wants to live in <font color="#10b981">**a better**</font> place.
- It is <font color="#10b981">**a much shorter**</font> way.

## Местоимения после than

- She has <font color="#10b981">**much more**</font> free time than me.
- He knows English <font color="#10b981">**better**</font> than her.
- He earns <font color="#10b981">**much less**</font> money than her.
- He is <font color="#10b981">**a little older**</font> than me.
- She is three years <font color="#10b981">**younger**</font> than me.
- This business is <font color="#10b981">**much more profitable**</font> than theirs.

## Превосходная степень

- It is <font color="#10b981">**the richest**</font> company in the world.
- She is <font color="#10b981">**the eldest**</font> sister.
- It is one of <font color="#10b981">**the easiest**</font> exercises.
- He is <font color="#10b981">**the best**</font> player in the team.
- It is one of <font color="#10b981">**the best**</font> hotels.

## Другие сравнительные конструкции

- It is one of <font color="#10b981">**the best**</font> movies I have ever watched.
- It is <font color="#10b981">**the most interesting**</font> movie I have ever watched.
- The exam <font color="#10b981">**isn't as easy as**</font> it looks.
- The problem is <font color="#10b981">**not as simple as**</font> it seems.
- This book is <font color="#10b981">**not as interesting as**</font> he expected.
- This exercise is <font color="#10b981">**a bit more difficult**</font> than the last one.
- This exercise is <font color="#10b981">**much more difficult**</font> than the previous one.
