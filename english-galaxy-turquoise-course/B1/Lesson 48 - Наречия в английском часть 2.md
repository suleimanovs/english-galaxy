##### significant — значительный
- Of course, it is a <font color="#10b981">**significant**</font> sum of money.
- It was such a <font color="#10b981">**significant**</font> step forward.

##### significantly — значительно
- I <font color="#10b981">**significantly**</font> improved my knowledge thanks to this course.

##### thanks to — благодаря кому-то/чему-то
- I significantly improved my knowledge <font color="#10b981">**thanks to**</font> this course.

##### frank — откровенный
- He was very <font color="#10b981">**frank**</font> with us talking about it.
- To be <font color="#10b981">**frank**</font> I expected much more.
- To be <font color="#10b981">**frank**</font> I didn't expect it.

##### frankly — откровенно
- I will say it quite <font color="#10b981">**frankly**</font>.

##### natural — природный, натуральный
- This food contains only <font color="#10b981">**natural**</font> ingredients.
- It is <font color="#10b981">**natural**</font> that most people can't afford to buy it.

##### naturally — естественно
- <font color="#10b981">**Naturally**</font> most people can't afford to buy it.
- I want to speak English more <font color="#10b981">**naturally**</font>.

##### easy — лёгкий
- I find this test relatively <font color="#10b981">**easy**</font>.

##### relatively easy — относительно лёгкий
- I find this test <font color="#10b981">**relatively easy**</font>.

##### easily — легко
- I solved all these problems pretty <font color="#10b981">**easily**</font>.

##### short — короткий

##### to be short — короче говоря
- <font color="#10b981">**To be short**</font> everything went smoothly.
- <font color="#10b981">**To be short**</font> everything went very well.

##### shortly — кратко
- I will say it quite <font color="#10b981">**shortly**</font>.

##### go smoothly — пройти гладко
- To be short everything <font color="#10b981">**went smoothly**</font>.

##### current — текущий
- What can we do in the <font color="#10b981">**current**</font> situation?

##### currently — в настоящее время
- This website is <font color="#10b981">**currently**</font> unavailable.
- He is <font color="#10b981">**currently**</font> working on another project.
