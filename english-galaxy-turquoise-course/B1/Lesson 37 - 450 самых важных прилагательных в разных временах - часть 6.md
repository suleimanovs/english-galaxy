##### amazing — удивительный, изумительный

- It is such an <font color="#10b981">**amazing**</font> opportunity.

##### potential — потенциальный

- It is a <font color="#10b981">**potential**</font> threat.
- There are several <font color="#10b981">**potential**</font> buyers.

##### threat — угроза

- It is a potential <font color="#10b981">**threat**</font>.

##### ridiculous — нелепый, абсурдный

- It is absolutely <font color="#10b981">**ridiculous**</font>.
- It seems absolutely <font color="#10b981">**ridiculous**</font>.

##### popular — популярный

##### unpopular — непопулярный

- It was a very <font color="#10b981">**unpopular**</font> decision at that time?

##### slight — небольшой

- There was a <font color="#10b981">**slight**</font> improvement in sales.

##### religious — религиозный

- It contradicts his <font color="#10b981">**religious**</font> beliefs.

##### professional — профессиональный

- He decided not to become a <font color="#10b981">**professional**</font> sportsman.

##### willing — желающий

- They are <font color="#10b981">**willing**</font> to come.

##### unwilling — не желающий

- They <font color="#10b981">**unwilling**</font> to talk about it.

##### responsible — ответственный

- He is <font color="#10b981">**responsible**</font> for this project.
- Who is <font color="#10b981">**responsible**</font> for it?

##### sudden — внезапный

- They weren't ready for these <font color="#10b981">**sudden**</font> changes.

##### ordinary — обычный

- These measures are taken to help <font color="#10b981">**ordinary**</font> people.

##### current — текущий

- What can we do in the <font color="#10b981">**current**</font> situation?

##### attractive — привлекательный

- This offer seems so <font color="#10b981">**attractive**</font>.
- It is such an <font color="#10b981">**attractive**</font> offer.
