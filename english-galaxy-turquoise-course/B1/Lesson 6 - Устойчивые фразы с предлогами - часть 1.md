##### for a while — на некоторое время

- I need to stay there <font color="#10b981">**for a while**</font>.

##### with the help of — с помощью

- I improve my English <font color="#10b981">**with the help of**</font> these lessons.
- I can improve my English <font color="#10b981">**with the help of**</font> this application.

##### for a change — для разнообразия

- We can go to another place <font color="#10b981">**for a change**</font>.

##### go for a walk — пойти на прогулку

- We can <font color="#10b981">**go for a walk**</font> now if you don't mind.

##### if you don't mind — если ты не возражаешь

- We can go for a walk now <font color="#10b981">**if you don't mind**</font>.

##### just out of curiosity — чисто (просто) из любопытства

- Let me ask you one question <font color="#10b981">**just out of curiosity**</font>.
- <font color="#10b981">**Just out of curiosity**</font>, how did you manage to do it?

##### at least — по крайней мере

- He wants to know <font color="#10b981">**at least**</font> two foreign languages.

##### for sale — продаётся, выставлен на продажу

- This house is <font color="#10b981">**for sale**</font>.

##### in theory — в теории

- It is very good only <font color="#10b981">**in theory**</font>.
- It is very good only <font color="#10b981">**in theory**</font> but not <font color="#10b981">**in practice**</font>.

##### in practice — на практике

- It is very good only in theory but not <font color="#10b981">**in practice**</font>.

##### with a view of — с видом на что-то

- I would like to have a room <font color="#10b981">**with a view of**</font> the sea.

##### by the way — кстати

- <font color="#10b981">**By the way**</font>, what are going to do in this situation?
- <font color="#10b981">**By the way**</font>, why did you choose this hotel?

##### for example · for instance — например

- <font color="#10b981">**For example**</font>, if you want to make a career, you can start working there for a start.
- <font color="#10b981">**For instance**</font>, you could try to spend your time more rationally.

##### make a career — сделать карьеру

- For example, if you want to <font color="#10b981">**make a career**</font>, you can start working there for a start.

##### for a start — для начала

- For example, if you want to make a career, you can start working there <font color="#10b981">**for a start**</font>.

##### in any case — в любом случае

- I know that I will have to do it <font color="#10b981">**in any case**</font>.

##### for ages — целую вечность

- I haven't seen you <font color="#10b981">**for ages**</font>.

##### at once — сразу

- He decided to do it <font color="#10b981">**at once**</font>.

##### in vain — напрасно

- All his efforts were <font color="#10b981">**in vain**</font>.

##### at a loss — в растерянности

- I was really <font color="#10b981">**at a loss**</font>.
- I'm really <font color="#10b981">**at a loss**</font>.

##### to a certain extent · to a certain degree — в какой-то степени, в некоторой степени, в определённой степени

- She is right <font color="#10b981">**to a certain extent**</font>.
- I was right <font color="#10b981">**to a certain extent**</font>.
- I can agree with you <font color="#10b981">**to a certain degree**</font>.

##### for sure — точно

- I know it <font color="#10b981">**for sure**</font>.
- That is <font color="#10b981">**for sure**</font>.
