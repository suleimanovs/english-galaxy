##### loyal — лояльный

- He is very <font color="#10b981">**loyal**</font> to them.

##### loyal to — лоялен к кому-то

- He is very <font color="#10b981">**loyal to**</font> them.

##### foolish — глупый, дурацкий

- What a <font color="#10b981">**foolish**</font> question!

##### fool — глупец, дурак

##### power — мощь, сила

##### powerful — мощный

- They have such a <font color="#10b981">**powerful**</font> organization.

##### unstable — нестабильный

- It is such an <font color="#10b981">**unstable**</font> situation.
- The situation seems very <font color="#10b981">**unstable**</font> nowadays.

##### precious — ценный

- Time is so <font color="#10b981">**precious**</font>.

##### excited — в восторге

##### excited about — в восторге от

- I'm really <font color="#10b981">**excited about**</font> this offer.

##### artificial — искусственный

- They used mostly <font color="#10b981">**artificial**</font> materials.

##### confused — смущён

##### confused about — смущён

- I'm really <font color="#10b981">**confused about**</font> it.

##### late for — опаздывать куда-то

- He was <font color="#10b981">**late for**</font> the meeting.
- She was <font color="#10b981">**late for**</font> the lesson.

##### natural — натуральный

- They usually use <font color="#10b981">**natural**</font> materials.

##### immediate — немедленный

- It was an <font color="#10b981">**immediate**</font> success.

##### attitude to / towards — отношение к чему-то

- What is your <font color="#10b981">**attitude to**</font> plastic surgery?

##### surgery — хирургия

##### worried about — взволнован, обеспокоен

- The workers are really <font color="#10b981">**worried about**</font> it.

##### whole — весь, вся

##### the whole truth — вся правда

- He told me the <font color="#10b981">**whole truth**</font>.

##### find out the whole truth — узнать всю правду

- She says that she'd like to <font color="#10b981">**find out the whole truth**</font>.
