## so — такой, такая, такое, такие

**so** усиливает прилагательное или наречие (so + прилагательное).

- This place is <font color="#10b981">**so**</font> beautiful.
- This food was <font color="#10b981">**so**</font> tasty.
- They were <font color="#10b981">**so**</font> hospitable.

## such — такой, такая, такое, такие

**such** усиливает существительное (such + (a/an) + существительное).

- I like <font color="#10b981">**such**</font> music.
- They are <font color="#10b981">**such**</font> hospitable people.
- I really like <font color="#10b981">**such**</font> weather.
- I really like <font color="#10b981">**such**</font> food.
- <font color="#10b981">**Such**</font> a thing can happen.
- <font color="#10b981">**Such**</font> a theory exists.
- Why did he say <font color="#10b981">**such**</font> a thing?
- It was <font color="#10b981">**such**</font> a cold day.
- It is <font color="#10b981">**such**</font> a useful resource.
- It is <font color="#10b981">**such**</font> a useful application.

## many / a lot of / lots of — много

- I have <font color="#10b981">**many**</font> friends.
- I have <font color="#10b981">**a lot of**</font> friends.
- I have <font color="#10b981">**lots of**</font> friends.
- He has <font color="#10b981">**lots of**</font> interesting ideas.
- She has <font color="#10b981">**lots of**</font> energy.

## so much / so many — так много

> [!tip] so much vs so many
> **so many** — с исчисляемыми существительными во множественном числе.
> **so much** — с неисчисляемыми существительными.

- I have <font color="#10b981">**so many**</font> questions.
- He has <font color="#10b981">**so many**</font> problems.
- You have <font color="#10b981">**so much**</font> enthusiasm.
- He has <font color="#10b981">**so much**</font> money.

## enough — достаточно

- These lessons are good <font color="#10b981">**enough**</font>.
- The quality of this product is good <font color="#10b981">**enough**</font>.
- The box is big <font color="#10b981">**enough**</font>.

## few / a few

> [!note] few vs a few
> **few** — мало (далее множественное число).
> **a few** — несколько.

- He has <font color="#10b981">**few**</font> friends.
- I have <font color="#10b981">**a few**</font> good friends.
- I have <font color="#10b981">**a few**</font> questions.
- He made some mistakes.
- She made <font color="#10b981">**a few**</font> mistakes.
- Unfortunately, <font color="#10b981">**few**</font> people understand it.
- Unfortunately, not so many people understand it.
- I want to make <font color="#10b981">**fewer**</font> mistakes.
- I feel much more confidence and I make much <font color="#10b981">**fewer**</font> mistakes than before.
- It was such a pleasure to meet you.
- It was such a pleasure to meet you at last.
