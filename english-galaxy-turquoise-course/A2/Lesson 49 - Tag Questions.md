## Введение в тему

**Tag question** (разделительный вопрос) — это короткий вопрос в конце предложения вроде «не так ли?», «правда?». Он образуется из вспомогательного глагола и местоимения.

> [!tip] Главное правило
> Если основное предложение **утвердительное**, хвостик будет **отрицательным**, и наоборот. Вспомогательный глагол в хвостике совпадает с временем и формой основного предложения.

## 1. Present Simple — обычный глагол (don't / doesn't)

- You know it, <font color="#10b981">**don't you**</font>? — Ты знаешь это, не так ли?
- You learn English by yourself, <font color="#10b981">**don't you**</font>?
- He really wants it, <font color="#10b981">**doesn't he**</font>?
- It seems really unbelievable, <font color="#10b981">**doesn't it**</font>?
- It doesn't help, <font color="#10b981">**does it**</font>?
- These things don't help, <font color="#10b981">**do they**</font>?

> [!note]
> Если утверждение идёт через отрицание, то в хвостике уже либо **do**, либо **does** (без отрицания).

## 2. Present Simple — глагол to be (isn't / aren't)

- You are really happy, <font color="#10b981">**aren't you**</font>?
- You are joking, <font color="#10b981">**aren't you**</font>?
- Learning English is very interesting, <font color="#10b981">**isn't it**</font>?
- It is really unbelievable, <font color="#10b981">**isn't it**</font>?
- I'm right, <font color="#10b981">**aren't I**</font>?

> [!tip] Почему здесь aren't I?
> Для «I am» в разделительном вопросе используется устоявшаяся форма **aren't I?** (форма «amn't I?» в английском не употребляется).

## 3. Past Simple — обычный глагол (didn't)

- I did the right thing, <font color="#10b981">**didn't I**</font>?
- He faced very serious problems, <font color="#10b981">**didn't he**</font>?
- He didn't go there, <font color="#10b981">**did he**</font>?
- She didn't help you, <font color="#10b981">**did she**</font>?

## 4. Past Simple — глагол to be (wasn't / weren't)

- She was late for the meeting, <font color="#10b981">**wasn't she**</font>?
- You were late for the meeting, <font color="#10b981">**weren't you**</font>?
- You weren't late, <font color="#10b981">**were you**</font>?

## 5. Future Simple — обычный глагол (won't)

- He will be with you, <font color="#10b981">**won't he**</font>?
- We won't know about it, <font color="#10b981">**will we**</font>?

## 6. Have got / has got

- You have got a car, <font color="#10b981">**haven't you**</font>?
- He has got a car, <font color="#10b981">**hasn't he**</font>?

## 7. Present Perfect (haven't / hasn't)

- You have already been there, <font color="#10b981">**haven't you**</font>?
- You have never been to this place, <font color="#10b981">**have you**</font>?

## 8. Present Perfect Continuous (haven't / hasn't)

- You have been working all day, <font color="#10b981">**haven't you**</font>?

## 9. Should / shouldn't

- I shouldn't do it, <font color="#10b981">**should I**</font>?

## 10. Let's — shall we

- Let's (let us) do it, <font color="#10b981">**shall we**</font>?
- Let's go there together, <font color="#10b981">**shall we**</font>?
- Let's take a taxi, <font color="#10b981">**shall we**</font>?
