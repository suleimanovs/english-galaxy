> Формат деловых и неформальных писем на английском отличается от русского. Здесь шаблоны для основных типов переписки.

---

## Formal email / letter (деловое письмо)

```
Subject: [Чёткая тема] -- Meeting Request / Application for... / Invoice Query

Dear Mr/Ms [Фамилия],        (если знаешь имя)
Dear Sir or Madam,            (если не знаешь)
Dear Hiring Manager,          (если пишешь на вакансию)
To Whom It May Concern,       (очень формально, крайний случай)

[Paragraph 1: причина письма]
I am writing to inquire about...
I am writing with regard to...
I am writing to apply for the position of...
I would like to inform you that...
Further to our conversation on [date], I am writing to...

[Paragraph 2-3: детали]
I would be grateful if you could...
Could you please confirm whether...
Please find attached...
I would like to draw your attention to...
With reference to your email dated...

[Final paragraph: следующий шаг]
I look forward to hearing from you.
Please do not hesitate to contact me if you require further information.
I would appreciate a response at your earliest convenience.
Thank you for your time and consideration.

Yours sincerely,              (если знаешь имя: Dear Mr Smith)
Yours faithfully,             (если не знаешь: Dear Sir or Madam)
Kind regards,                 (нейтрально-формальный)
Best regards,                 (нейтрально-формальный)

[Имя Фамилия]
[Должность]
[Контакт]
```

---

## Semi-formal email (полуформальное)

Для коллег, клиентов с которыми уже общался, преподавателей.

```
Subject: Quick question about the project

Hi [Имя],
Hello [Имя],
Dear [Имя],

[Тело письма -- короче, проще, но всё ещё вежливо]

Thanks for your help.
Let me know if you have any questions.
Looking forward to your reply.

Best,
Best regards,
Many thanks,
Cheers, (British, неформально-дружелюбный)

[Имя]
```

---

## Informal email / message (неформальное)

Для друзей, близких знакомых.

```
Hey [Имя]! / Hi! / Hiya!

[Свободный текст]

Catch you later!
Talk soon!
Take care!
Love, [Имя]
xx
```

## Полезные фразы по ситуациям

### Запрос информации

| Formal | Semi-formal |
|---|---|
| I am writing to inquire about... | I was wondering if you could tell me about... |
| I would be grateful if you could provide... | Could you send me some info on...? |
| Could you kindly confirm whether... | Do you happen to know if...? |

### Просьба

| Formal | Semi-formal |
|---|---|
| I would appreciate it if you could... | Would you mind...? |
| Would it be possible to...? | Could you possibly...? |
| I wonder if you would be so kind as to... | Any chance you could...? |

### Жалоба

| Formal | Semi-formal |
|---|---|
| I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with... | I'm not happy about... |
| I wish to draw your attention to... | I wanted to let you know that... |
| I must insist that this matter be resolved... | Could you look into this? |
| I expect a full refund / replacement. | I'd appreciate it if you could sort this out. |

### Извинение

| Formal | Semi-formal |
|---|---|
| I sincerely apologize for... | I'm really sorry about... |
| Please accept my apologies for... | Sorry for the mix-up / delay / confusion. |
| I deeply regret any inconvenience caused. | My bad, I should have... |

### Благодарность

| Formal | Semi-formal |
|---|---|
| I am extremely grateful for your assistance. | Thanks so much for your help! |
| Thank you for your prompt response. | Thanks for getting back to me so quickly. |
| I truly appreciate your time and effort. | Really appreciate it! |

### Приложения

| Formal | Semi-formal |
|---|---|
| Please find attached the requested documents. | I've attached the file. |
| Enclosed please find... | Here's the document you asked for. |
| As per your request, I am attaching... | Sending this over as promised. |

### Завершение и follow-up

| Formal | Semi-formal |
|---|---|
| I look forward to your response. | Let me know what you think. |
| Please do not hesitate to contact me. | Feel free to reach out if you need anything. |
| I would appreciate a reply by [date]. | It would be great to hear back by [date]. |

## Структура cover letter (сопроводительное письмо к резюме)

```
Dear [Hiring Manager / Mr/Ms Фамилия],

Paragraph 1: Позиция + где нашёл + почему интересна
I am writing to apply for the [position] role at [company],
as advertised on [source]. I am particularly drawn to this
opportunity because...

Paragraph 2: Опыт и навыки (конкретные примеры)
In my current role at [company], I have [achievement].
For example, I [specific result with numbers if possible].

Paragraph 3: Почему именно эта компания
I admire [company]'s commitment to [value/mission].
I believe my experience in [area] would allow me to
contribute to [specific goal].

Paragraph 4: Заключение
I would welcome the opportunity to discuss my application
further. I am available for an interview at your convenience.
Thank you for considering my application.

Yours sincerely,
[Имя Фамилия]
```

## Типичные ошибки русскоязычных

| Ошибка | Правильно |
|---|---|
| "Dear friend" в формальном письме | Dear Mr Smith / Dear Sir or Madam |
| "Respectfully, ..." как завершение | Yours sincerely / Kind regards |
| Длинное вступление перед сутью | Первый абзац сразу объясняет цель письма |
| "I want to..." в просьбе | "I would like to..." / "I would appreciate if..." |
| Отсутствие Subject в email | Всегда писать чёткий Subject |
| "With best wishes" в деловом письме | Kind regards / Best regards |
| Слишком длинные абзацы | 3-5 предложений на абзац максимум |
